FAQs

Can I make an additional cash or credit donation?

Yes, were accepting additional card and cash donations. If youd like to make a food donation, please check with your local food bank!

Where does my money go?

$1 from every drink sold is donated to local organizations to help feed our communities. Ask your local broista which organization your shop is supporting!

Is Dutch Luv Day all day long and at all shops?

Of course! Were celebrating Dutch Luv Day company-wide on Feb. 17. Ask your local broista which organization your favorite shop is supporting!

Why cant I drop food off at my local shop?

Food banks are better equipped to handle and distribute food donations. Please check with your local organization if you want to donate more!

How much has Dutch Bros donated from Dutch Luv Day?

Last year, we donated more than $800,000. We're stoked to make a difference together each year!

When is Dutch Luv Day?

This year, help us support local organizations feeding your community on Friday, Feb. 17, 2023!

When did Dutch Bros start Dutch Luv Day?

Dutch Luv Day started in 2007 as a canned food drive at shops throughout the company. In 2016, Dutch Luv transitioned from a canned food drive to a giveback day in which $1 from each drink sold at every shop goes to local organizations to help feed our communities.

Is there a promotion on Dutch Luv Day?

We dont have any specials planned this day, but a special sticker will be available at shops AND $1 from every drink sold helps provide meals to local families, allowing all of our communities to join us in making a massive difference one cup at a time.

Why isnt Dutch Luv Day on Valentines Day this year?

We wanted to keep the love going- why celebrate on one day, when you can celebrate on two?

How does Dutch Bros choose an organization to donate to?

Local management chooses the non-profit theyd like to support on Dutch Luv Day. Theyre encouraged to find an organization making a meaningful difference in providing meals for our communities.

Whats Dutch Luv Day?

Dutch Luv Day is one of our three company-wide givebacks! Local management chooses a local non-profit organization helping feed their community and $1 from every drink sold is donated to the organization of choice.
